About Us

Bread and Butter Boulangerie was started in 2010 by avid bread bakers Henry and Lauren. They had a vision to craft hearty, wholesome breads never made with unhealthy or bad-for-you ingredients. A vision was born, and Bread and Butter Boulangerie has now expanded to offer a variety of breads and a warm, welcoming experience for our customers.


"Never harmful, always hearty."
